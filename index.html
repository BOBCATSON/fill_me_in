About Us

Here at FMIF, we provide a unique taste of London set to the backdrop of (Gallery name) Gallery.

Our menu changes daily as we always use the freshest ingredients, all sourced from local markets in the Dalston area.

We smoke all our meats overnight for maximum flavour and taste.

Our venue is quirky and unique, with both feasting and more intimate tables for any occasion.

We serves smoked meats, fish, cheese and vegetables along with a selection of stimulating salads.
