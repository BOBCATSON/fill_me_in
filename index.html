About Us

FILL ME IN is Hackney’s latest culinary sensation, located at the all new Hackney Creative’s Social Club.

Our menu changes daily as we always use the freshest ingredients, all sourced from local markets in the Dalston area.

We provide a variety of smoked meats as well as using the smoking process to create enticing vegetarian and vegan options, packed with powerful flavours that are only possible through the art of slow cooking.

Our venue is quirky and unique, located in the heart of East London’s creative hub, FILL ME IN offers a casual, relaxed environment offering brunch, lunch, snacks and coffees.

Our food

Our menu changes daily, depending on what’s in season from our carefully selected suppliers, but you’ll always find something fresh from our Blackwood smoker.

We smoke all our meats overnight, using a variety of wood chips and smoking techniques to enrich our food with maximum flavour and taste.

  • SMOKED MEATS
  • VEGAN & VEGGIE DELIGHTS
  • STIMULATING SALADS
  • SOUPS
  • SWEET TREATS
  • COFFEE, TEA & SOFT DRINKS

Events
