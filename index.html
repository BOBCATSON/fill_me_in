About Us

FILL ME IN is Dalston’s newest culinary experience.

Through the art of slow cooking, our smoker creates a delicious variety of food, using flavours from all corners of the world.

We make mouth-watering treats in a quirky and unique setting, where food and art come alive.

Food & Ethos

Our menu changes daily, depending on what’s in season from our carefully selected suppliers, but you’ll always find something fresh from our Blackwood smoker.

We smoke all of our meats overnight, using a variety of wood chips and smoking techniques to enrich our food with maximum flavour and taste.

  • SMOKED MEATS
  • VEGAN & VEGGIE DELIGHTS
  • STIMULATING SALADS
  • SOUPS
  • SWEET TREATS
  • COFFEE, TEA & SOFT DRINKS

Events & Venue Hire

We cater for private hire events from casual to corporate, including birthday parties, meeting space and private functions.

We offering a unique setting, as well as customised menu's specific to your requirements.

Regular events and exhibitions are always on at Hackney Creative Social Club, as well as within the restaurant space itself. We are always looking to provide local, up-and-coming artists the opportunity to showcase their work.

If you are interested in hiring the space, or need more information about our services then drop us an email and we will get back to you.
